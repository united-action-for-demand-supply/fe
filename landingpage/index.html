We turn good intentions into valuable acts of giving in times of crisis.

The general public wants to help. We want to make it possible to connect the wider public to MSB, Socialstyrelsen and other public authorities. To channel volunteering efforts and make it easier for MSB to safely and correctly allocate acts of giving to those in need, through a scalable, distributed, platform that connects the peers. In this crisis.

The Aiditto team (40 teammates) was formed organically during the Hack The Crisis Sweden Hackathon. We became an inseparable digital family, distributed across Sweden and Finland. We have about 20 very active members (post-hackathon) and about 20 additional people who are actively engaged in our cause. A diverse, engaged and skilled team of doers.

We seek a first round of project funding to detail the project, and size it, to then get the remaining funding in place during May. We want to support the current outbreak where we can, and build strong readyness for the second wave.
